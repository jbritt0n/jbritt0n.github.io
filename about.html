About

Jonathan Britton

Painter · Grand Rapids, Michigan

I make paintings. Mostly oil, though I've been known to reach for gouache, acrylic, charcoal, encaustic, collage, and linocut when the mood is right or the oil is taking too long to dry.

I'm not sure I have a single unifying vision — or if I do, I haven't found the words for it yet. What I do know is that I like making things, and I like the particular quietness that happens when a painting starts working, which is rare enough to feel worth chasing.

This site is mostly a place to put the work so that galleries and other people can see it. I don't take myself too seriously, but I do take the work seriously, which feels like the right balance.

I'm available for commissions, questions, and the occasional rambling email. Works can be purchased directly — see the contact page for how that works.
